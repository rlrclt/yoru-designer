# Global Design Disciplines: Fields and Suitability

> "Design is the bridge between human need and structural reality."

This document categorizes the major branches of design worldwide and identifies the types of work each branch is best suited for. For UI-O, this provides the broader ecosystem context for Frontend Architecture.

## 1. Physical & Spatial Design (The World Builders)

### Architectural Design
- **Suitability**: Built environments, long-term human habitation.
- **Fields**:
    - **Residential**: Private homes, focus on domestic comfort.
    - **Commercial**: Offices, retail, focus on flow and brand.
    - **Industrial**: Factories, focus on operational safety and logistics.

### Urban Design & Landscape Architecture
- **Suitability**: City-scale planning, outdoor ecosystems.
- **Fields**: Public parks, city layouts, infrastructure integration.

### Interior Design
- **Suitability**: Human-scale interaction within spaces.
- **Fields**: Residential, commercial interiors, material specification.

## 2. Industrial & Object Design (The Tool Makers)

### Industrial (Product) Design
- **Suitability**: Physical goods, mass production.
- **Fields**: Electronics, furniture, medical devices, automotive.
- **Focus**: Ergonomics, manufacturing constraints, aesthetics of objects.

### Fashion & Textile Design
- **Suitability**: Human body interaction, material expression.
- **Fields**: Apparel, high fashion, wearable tech.

## 3. Communication & Digital Design (The Information Architects)

### Graphic Design
- **Suitability**: Visual communication, branding, identity.
- **Fields**: Print, logos, advertisements, typography.

### UI/UX & Digital Product Design (UI-O's Domain)
- **Suitability**: Human-computer interaction, digital systems.
- **Fields**: Web apps, mobile apps, software interfaces.
- **Focus**: Usability, structural integrity, information flow, design systems.

### Motion & Game Design
- **Suitability**: Temporal and interactive storytelling.
- **Fields**: Animation, video games, interactive entertainment.

## 4. Strategic & Systemic Design (The Logic Architects)

### Service Design
- **Suitability**: Intangible experiences, multi-touchpoint journeys.
- **Fields**: Banking services, healthcare flows, public sector systems.

### Instructional (Learning) Design
- **Suitability**: Knowledge transfer, educational efficiency.
- **Fields**: E-learning, corporate training, educational curricula.

### Systems Design
- **Suitability**: High-complexity, interconnected systems.
- **Fields**: Engineering systems, organizational structures, software architecture.
